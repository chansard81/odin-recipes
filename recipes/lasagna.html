Lasagna

Description

Classic lasagna made with an easy meat sauce base. Just layer the sauce with the noodles and cheese and bake until bubbly. This is great for a large family or freezing to eat later!

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 28oz. can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 14oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch gralic powder or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar, to taste
  • salt

To assemble Lasagna

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces Ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/4 pund freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell peppers, onions, garlic and add to the beef
  4. Transfer to medium pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce.
  5. Boil and drain the pasta noodles
  6. Heat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
